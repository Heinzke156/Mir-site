The Mir space station

The core module of the Mir space station was launched on
the 20th of Febuary 1986 after almost exactly a decade of
development. Over the course of the next 15 years it would
be visited by 104 people from 12 different countries and
be expanded to have 350m2 of living and working space
One cosmonaut, Valeri Polyakov, spent 437 days on board
and was one of few who for a time, could call Mir their
home
